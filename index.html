About Me

Hello! My name is Chris Ferenci. I am pursuing my Master's degree in Information Management and Systems at the University of California, Berkeley.

Over the past 7 years, I have worked mostly as a Product and User Experience designer. My journey in technology started in 2015 as the Creative Director for Mobile First Studios, a digital agency specializing in the design and development of consumer web and mobile products. One year later in 2016, I joined environmental and geospatial software giant ESRI, desiging and researching user experiences across their entire mobile suite. Then, from 2017 to 2021, I led design efforts at TicketManager, an event management and client entertainment SaaS solution. There I pioneered a full platform redesign across their web, mobile, and Salesforce products.

Now, at University of California, Berkeley, I'm studying management and emerging technologies in the health, biosensing, and wearables space. The future of wearable technology is incredibly exciting, and I believe there remain many opportunities for designers to make an impact.
